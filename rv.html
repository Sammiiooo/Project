RABBIT VACCINATION

It is important to vaccinate your rabbit to ensure it lives a long and healthy life. Rabbit vaccines help the rabbit to combat the incubation and transmission of fatal diseases. It also helps the humans as a bite or scratch from the rabbit won't turn infectious and can be just washed off.

To be fully protected, rabbits need to be vaccinated against Myxomatosis and both types of viral haemorrhagic disease (RHD1 and RHD2). This can now be done with a single injection.

Myxo RHD PLUS is now available and is a combined vaccine for all three of these potentially fatal diseases. This means that your rabbit only needs a single vaccination to be fully protected.

Your Rabbit needs to be vaccinates once annually with the Myxo RHD PLUS.
